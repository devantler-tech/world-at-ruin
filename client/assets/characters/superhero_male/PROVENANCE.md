# Provenance — Superhero Male base body

The first committed art asset, sanctioned by the maintainer's stage-2 direction on
[#20](https://github.com/devantler-tech/world-at-ruin/issues/20): *"a committed CC0 rigged base
mesh + bone-scale proportion parametrization in `@tool` GDScript"* — tried before spending the
Blender/Python exception.

- **Asset:** Universal Base Characters — Superhero Male full body (glTF "Godot - UE" export)
- **Author:** Quaternius
- **Licence:** CC0 1.0 (public domain) — stated on the author's own page:
  <https://quaternius.com/packs/universalbasecharacters.html> ("Free to use in personal,
  educational and commercial projects. (CC0 License)", linking to
  <https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/>). Verified 2026-07-17.
- **Fetched:** 2026-07-17, `Universal Base Characters[Standard].zip` (itch.io official download,
  <https://quaternius.itch.io/universal-base-characters>), pack zip
  sha256 `fdbf1804c90dfc1ea03e992bff7da2dfd1a79318e13270a660180f9308455f40`.
- **Files:** byte-identical copies from the pack's `Base Characters/Godot - UE/` directory.
  Two textures are committed under the URI names the glTF references
  (`T_Hair_1_Normal_png.png` ← `T_Hair_1_Normal.png`, `T_Eye_Normal_png.png` ←
  `T_Eye_Normal.png`) — the exporter wrote `_png`-suffixed URIs; contents are unmodified.
- **Contents:** 65-bone humanoid rig (UE-style names), skinned meshes: body 12 566 tris,
  eyes 768, eyebrows 984. T-pose rest. No animations (the generator poses it).
